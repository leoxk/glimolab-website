## Project Background

TrendSet Apparel, a fast-fashion brand targeting Gen Z across Southeast Asia, needed a high-volume short-form video campaign to promote their new summer collection. The goal: create enough content to post daily across TikTok and Instagram Reels for an entire month.

The brand needed variety — different styles, hooks, and trends — to test what resonated with their audience. Traditional production could never deliver this volume within budget.

## Our Solution

We deployed our batch production pipeline optimized for short-form social content:

1. **Trend Analysis** — We analyzed current TikTok trends, audio patterns, and viral formats to inform the creative direction.
2. **Batch Creative Generation** — AI generated 30 unique short-form video concepts, each with different hooks, transitions, and visual styles.
3. **Rapid Production** — All 30 videos were generated, edited, and exported in just 3 days, with each video tailored to specific TikTok trends.
4. **A/B Testing Variants** — For key products, we produced 3-4 creative variants to test which performed best.
5. **Platform-Native Export** — Each video was exported in 9:16 format with platform-optimized metadata and captions.

## Results

The campaign was a viral success. Across 30 videos posted over 30 days, the campaign generated **5.8 million combined views** on TikTok and Instagram Reels. The brand saw a **52% increase** in website traffic from social media and a significant boost in summer collection sales.

The total production cost was **75% lower** than what traditional short-form video production would have required for the same volume, and TrendSet now orders monthly batches of 20-30 videos.
