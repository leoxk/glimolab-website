## Project Background

ShopEase, a leading e-commerce seller in Southeast Asia, needed to produce a massive volume of product showcase videos for their quarterly Shopee Mega Sale campaign. With 50 products to promote and only 48 hours before the sale went live, traditional video production was not an option.

The challenge: create 50 unique, high-quality product videos that would stand out in a crowded marketplace — each optimized for Shopee's video format and tailored to the specific product category.

## Our Solution

We deployed our AI video production pipeline to handle the scale:

1. **Batch Script Generation** — AI analyzed product specs and generated compelling scripts for all 50 products in under 2 hours.
2. **Parallel AI Generation** — Using Seedance 2.0 and Kling, we generated video footage for all products simultaneously, cutting traditional production time from weeks to hours.
3. **Automated Voiceover** — ElevenLabs provided natural-sounding voiceovers in both English and Filipino, tailored to each product's target audience.
4. **Platform Optimization** — Every video was automatically exported in Shopee's required format with optimized thumbnails.

## Results

The campaign was a resounding success. The 50 videos generated **2.5 million combined views** during the Mega Sale weekend, driving a **45% increase in conversion rate** compared to the previous quarter. Most importantly, the entire production cost **80% less** than what traditional video production would have required.

ShopEase has since become a retainer client, ordering 20+ videos per week for ongoing campaigns.
