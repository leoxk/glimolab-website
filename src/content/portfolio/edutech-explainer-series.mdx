## Project Background

LearnSphere, an online education platform, wanted to create a series of explainer videos covering fundamental AI and data science concepts. The series needed to make complex topics accessible to beginners while maintaining professional production quality.

With 12 topics to cover and a tight production budget, traditional animation studios would have taken months and exceeded the budget significantly.

## Our Solution

We leveraged our AI pipeline to produce animated explainer content at scale:

1. **Script Development** — Our team collaborated with LearnSphere's subject matter experts to write clear, engaging scripts for all 12 episodes.
2. **AI Motion Graphics** — Using AI generation tools, we created animated sequences that visually explained abstract concepts like neural networks, gradient descent, and data pipelines.
3. **Consistent Visual Style** — AI ensured visual consistency across all 12 videos, maintaining LearnSphere's brand guidelines throughout.
4. **Multi-language Subtitles** — Whisper automatically generated subtitles in English, Filipino, and Bahasa Indonesia for each video.
5. **Iterative Refinement** — The AI pipeline allowed LearnSphere to request changes to specific scenes without re-shooting or re-animating from scratch.

## Results

The 12-video series launched as part of LearnSphere's free introductory course. It accumulated **890,000 views** across YouTube and their platform in the first quarter, and contributed to a **28% increase** in course completion rates.

The project was delivered in 2 weeks at **70% lower cost** than traditional animation, and LearnSphere has commissioned a second series covering advanced topics.
