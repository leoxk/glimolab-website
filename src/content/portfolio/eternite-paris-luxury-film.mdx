## Concept

This concept case uses a luxury fragrance film format: warm Paris architecture, close-up product moments, and a nostalgic cinematic tone. It is designed for brands that need a premium hero asset for launch pages, retail screens, social cutdowns, or high-end campaign storytelling.

## Production Direction

The creative direction focuses on three priorities:

1. **Product-first storytelling** with the bottle kept as the visual anchor.
2. **Cinematic atmosphere** through golden-hour lighting, soft camera movement, and editorial pacing.
3. **Brand memory** built around a simple emotional hook rather than dense product explanation.
