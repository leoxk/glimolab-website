## Project Background

Nova Finance, a rapidly growing fintech startup in the Philippines, was preparing for their Series A funding announcement. They needed a compelling brand story video that would communicate their mission of financial inclusion while showcasing their innovative product suite.

Traditional production houses quoted 3 weeks and a substantial budget. Nova needed the video in 5 days for their launch event.

## Our Solution

We combined AI generation with creative direction to deliver a cinematic brand story:

1. **Creative Concept** — Our Creative Director developed a storyboard blending Nova's brand narrative with AI-visualizable scenes.
2. **AI Cinematic Generation** — Using Seedance 2.0, we generated cinematic footage of diverse Southeast Asian communities, cityscapes, and technology interfaces.
3. **Professional Voiceover** — ElevenLabs produced a warm, authoritative voiceover that conveyed Nova's brand personality.
4. **Motion Graphics** — We overlaid brand-consistent motion graphics for data visualizations and product UI showcases.
5. **Multi-format Delivery** — Delivered as a 2-minute hero video, plus 15-second and 30-second cut-downs for social media.

## Results

The brand story video premiered at Nova's Series A event and was subsequently deployed across YouTube, LinkedIn, and Instagram. It garnered **1.2 million views** in the first month and contributed to a **32% increase** in sign-ups following the launch.

The entire production was completed in 4 days at **65% lower cost** than traditional production, with unlimited revisions enabled by the AI pipeline.
