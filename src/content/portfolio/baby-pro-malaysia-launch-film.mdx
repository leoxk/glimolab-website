## Concept

This showcase presents Baby Pro entering Malaysia through a bright, character-led travel story. The direction uses a playful animated tone, tropical scenery, and clear brand presence to make the market-entry message immediately understandable.

## Production Direction

The video is structured around three goals:

1. **Character recognition** through recurring Baby Pro mascots and a friendly visual style.
2. **Regional context** with Malaysia-inspired travel and island scenery.
3. **Launch clarity** by keeping the story focused on one simple message: Baby Pro is now in Malaysia.

## Output Fit

Best suited for brand expansion announcements, social launch posts, retailer presentations, and short campaign teasers where the audience needs to understand the market story quickly.

## Notes

This is a brand showcase video for portfolio presentation. It focuses on creative direction, character continuity, and launch storytelling rather than public campaign performance metrics.
